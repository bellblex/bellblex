## 🌟 Interests

My interests span across a variety of areas. I'm fascinated by low-level programming, computer architecture, and server-side development, which let me dive deep into how systems work. At the same time, I enjoy web development and design, where I get to blend creativity with functionality.

In my free time, I enjoy playing computer games, binge-watching series, and drawing. It might sound cliché, but I also love learning new things, especially in my field of work. I enjoy experimenting with new technologies, like building a home server using parts from an old laptop and a second-hand workstation. These projects are a fun way to satisfy my curiosity and keep learning.

To balance my active mind with physical health, I enjoy playing futsal with friends and swimming. I’ve played federated futsal and competed in several swimming events growing up, which gave me some of my best memories and taught me valuable teamwork and discipline.
